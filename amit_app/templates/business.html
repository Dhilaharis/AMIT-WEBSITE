Business



AMIT is the leading manufacturer and distributor of Solar Inverter both On grid and Off grid, DC and AC regulated power supplies, Electronic Components (Inductor, Capacitor, Resistor, Rheostat, Transformer, Diodes, MOSFET, IGBT), Wireless Embedded IoT system, Sensors, Monitoring and control of Smart grid, Microgrid, Industrial motor drives, Home and Industrial Automation.
