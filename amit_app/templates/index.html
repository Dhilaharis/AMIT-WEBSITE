ABOUT AMIT


We Provide Top Industrial Services


Athith Mithra Industrial Technologies (AMIT) Private Limited manufactures a wide range of electronics components such as Resistor, Inductor, Capacitor, Diodes, electronic switches, Electronic Cables (RMC Cable), Driver Circuit, Small rating Transformer, Micro Controller (Arduino, PIC, DSPIC, Raspberry), Digital Signal Processor (DSP), Field Programmable Gate Array Controller (FPGA) and Artificial Intelligence based Robotics applications.

AMIT focuses specifically on Renewable energy sector and manufactures DC-DC Converter for Solar and Battery system, Single phase and three phase Industrial Drives, FACTS devices, Solar based UPS system and Wind Energy Generators.

AMIT shares the real time Industrial knowledge by conducting In-Plant Training and Internship to the students across various disciplines.
